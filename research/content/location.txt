Title: Visit Free Geek | Free Geek

Free Geek is located at:

1731 SE 10th Ave
Portland, OR 97214

We are between SE Market and Mill on 10th Ave, two blocks south of Hawthorne & seven blocks north of Division.

By bus

We're accessible via the 2, 6, 10, 14, and 70 TriMet bus lines

By bike

We have plenty of bike parking available

By car

You'll find free street parking around our building

Where to Go
Donors: Enter via the door marked ‘Donations’ near the north end of the block.
Volunteers, class and tour attendees: Enter via the door marked ‘Info & Volunteering.’
Free Geek Store shoppers: The store is located near the south end of the block.
